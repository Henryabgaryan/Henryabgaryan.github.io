Bio

PhD Student at Lamsade LAB, PSL Dauphine, Paris, France.

I specialize in advanced machine learning techniques for NP-hard combinatorial optimization problems. My research focuses on large language models, reinforcement learning, and generative AI approaches to optimization. My background spans text-to-image synthesis, extensive R&D endeavors, teaching responsibilities, and industry roles in deep learning.

Research Areas: Machine Learning, LLMs, RL for Combinatorial Optimization
