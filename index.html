Bio

Hi I am a PhD Student at Lamsade LAB, PSL Dauphine, Paris, France.

I am researching advanced machine learning techniques on NP-Hard combinatorial optimization problems. I specialize in advancing machine learning techniques to tackle NP-hard combinatorial optimization challenges. My background spans text-to-image synthesis, extensive R&D endeavors, teaching responsibilities, and industry roles in deep learning.
